Publications Types


Issue Briefs Describes analysis with new information and policy implications. Partners are involved in the process of drafting the issue brief. The target audience includes community partners, state and local decision-makers, and the public at large.


Snapshots: Provides a condensed version of an analysis and includes policy implications.


Policy Fact Sheets: Focused on a particular policy or research topic (e.g., dropouts).


Reports: Pieces generally reporting on Gardner Center research (often implementation or documentation research of a specific initiative) requested by a partner.
