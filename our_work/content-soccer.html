Soccer for Success


Partner Organizations

  • U.S. Soccer Foundation

Soccer for Success is a U.S. Soccer Foundation after‐school program that uses soccer to promote positive outcomes for children in low‐income urban communities. This study focused on Soccer for Success as implemented by the United Soccer Club (USC), the organization that runs Soccer for Success in Washington, DC. The research aimed to understand how the program is implemented and to uncover key conditions that facilitate or hinder the program’s ability to reach its goals. It employed a mixed methods design, including observations, interviews, focus group interviews, and student surveys, as well as specific measures of student outcomes. The findings provide preliminary evidence that Soccer for Success is an effective approach to addressing childhood obesity and juvenile delinquency among disadvantaged children in urban communities – communities deeply impacted by poverty and characterized by a scarcity of quality programs and services that promote the well‐being of their children and youth.
