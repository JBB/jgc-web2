BY TRAIN


Taking Caltrain to Stanford


From the Palo Alto Caltrain stop, free Marguerite shuttles will take you to campus. Take the Palm Drive shuttle (Line P) and get off at the Oval.

Walking to Barnum Center from the Oval
  • Face the Main Quad.
  • Turn left and walk down Serra Mall.
  • Turn left on Lasuen Street.
  • The Littlefield Center will be directly ahead.
  • Use the West Lobby doors to the left of the archway to access the elevator to the Gardner Center's office on the third floor.
