BY CAR


Driving to the Stanford campus


  • Parking Option 1: The closest visitor parking is in the Tresidder Union lot, #L-39 (699 Lagunita Drive, Stanford, CA).
  • Parking Option 2: Alternate parking is available in Parking Structure #6 (560 Wilbur Way, Stanford, CA).

Google Directions



Parking Option 1: Tresidder Union lot, #L-39 (699 Lagunita Drive, Stanford, CA)

Metered and "Pay and Display" parking are available at the Tresidder Union parking lot for a cost of $1.50 per hour. Parking meters require quarters and have a two-hour limit. "Pay and Display" machines accept Visa or MasterCard, cash, or coins, but do not issue change. Please be sure to place the "Pay and Display" receipt on your dashboard. Parking is FREE after 4:00 pm and during weekends.


    Walking from Tresidder Union parking lot to Barnum Center
    1. Head north toward the center of campus with Tresidder Union on your left and Dinkelspiel Auditorium on your right.
    2. Continue north on Lasuen Mall with the Old Union on your left and the Bookstore on your right.
    3. Barnum Center is on your right before reaching the Clock Tower at 505 Lasuen Mall.

Parking Option 2: Parking Structure #6 (560 Wilbur Way, Stanford, CA)

"Pay By Space Number" parking is available in Parking Structure #6 for a cost of $1.50 per hour. Park in a "Visitor Pay Parking" numbered spot (1-41 and 51-73). The "Pay By Space Number" machines accept Visa or MasterCard, cash, or coins, but do not issue change. You do not need to display your receipt. Parking is FREE after 4:00 pm and during weekends.


    Walking from Parking Structure #6 to Barnum Center
    1. Exit the parking structure and turn left on Bowdoin Lane.
    2. Cross Galvez Street and continue between the Law School (on your left) and CERAS (on your right).
    3. Head diagonally across Canfield Court toward the Clock Tower, keeping the Bookstore to your left.
    4. Enter through the back entrance of Barnum Center and walk through the building to JGC.

BY TRAIN


Taking Caltrain to Stanford

From the Palo Alto Caltrain stop, free Marguerite shuttles will take you to campus. Take the Palm Drive shuttle (Line P) and get off at the Oval.

Walking to Barnum Center from the Oval
  • Face the Main Quad.
  • Turn left and walk down Serra Mall.
  • Turn right on Lasuen Mall and walk the length of the Main Quad.
  • Pass the School of Education (Cubberley) and the Clock Tower on your left.
  • Barnum Center is on your left at 505 Lasuen Mall.
