Maryam Attai

Graduate Research Assistant

Maryam Lucia Attai is a doctoral student in Developmental and Psychological Sciences at the Stanford Graduate School of Education. Her research interests lie in the development of academic, civic, and moral purpose within collaborative spaces, primarily related to educational organizations and institutions. Prior to attending Stanford, Maryam received her bachelors in International Development with a minor in Geographic Information Systems and Technology, as well as a masters degree in Education in the field of Prevention Science and Practice from the Harvard Graduate School of Education. Throughout her academic career, Maryam has worked with the Junior Youth Spiritual Empowerment Program, a Baha'i inspired global initiative aimed at endowing youth ages 11-14 with the ability to recognize the spiritual significance of their actions and words by helping them form a strong moral identity and empowering them to contribute to the well-being of their communities and the world at large.
