Naomi Chapman

Community Engagement Associate

Naomi came to the Gardner Center as a Community Engagement Associate in December 2013 from the San Francisco Unified School District where, as a site nutrition coordinator, she helped develop and guide a framework for the district’s nutrition-focused youth participatory research projects.


Naomi has dedicated her career to supporting the health and wellbeing of youth and their families throughout the Bay Area. In her two years with AmeriCorps, she was a program assistant for BUILD and was “Coach Naomi” on the play yard for Playworks. As the Health and Wellness Youth Development Coordinator at StarVista, she energized young women to make healthy choices. At Redwood Continuation High School, she collaborated with the Teenage Parenting Program (TAPP) and community-based organizations in Redwood City to create cooking, nutrition, music, and exercise opportunities to improve the well-being and health outcomes of the low-income young mothers in TAPP.


Naomi holds a BA in Feminist Studies from the University of California Santa Cruz and an MPH from San Francisco State University.
