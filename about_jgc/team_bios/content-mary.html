Mary Hofstedt, Ed.M.

Senior Community Engagement Associate

Mary Hofstedt is a Senior Community Engagement Associate at the Gardner Center. Mary has worked with the Gardner Center for more than a decade, developing youth leadership initiatives, serving as an advisor in youth development research and practice, and directing cross-sector projects to improve outcomes for youth and communities. From 2003 to 2006, Mary directed the Youth Engaged in Leadership and Learning (YELL) program in Redwood City, and provided training in youth leadership development to regional and national practitioners as well as to Stanford students. Today, Mary provides cross-sector facilitation and youth development research and practice consultation for Bay Area organizations and collaboratives, with a specific focus in San Mateo County communities. Mary holds a masters degree from the Stanford University School of Education, and has a background in education and community development that includes nonprofit management, strategic planning, organizational development, fundraising, curriculum development and program design.
