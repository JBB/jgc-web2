Mary Hofstedt, Ed.M.

Senior Community Engagement Associate

Mary Hofstedt is a Senior Community Engagement Associate at the Gardner Center. Mary directed the Youth Engaged in Leadership and Learning (YELL) program in Redwood City from 2003 to 2006, and has trained program staff, mentors, and Stanford undergraduate and graduate students in Youth Development theory and practice. She also provides facilitation and youth development consultation for Bay Area organizations. Mary holds a masters degree from the Stanford University School of Education, and has a background in education ranging from non-profit management and fund raising to teaching and curriculum design.
