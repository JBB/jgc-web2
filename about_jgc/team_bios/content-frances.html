Frances Miller

Policy Analyst

Frances Miller joined the Gardner Center as a Policy Analyst in June 2014. Prior to joining the team, she was a research analyst in SRI International's Center for Education Policy. Through her work at SRI and the Consortium on Chicago School Research at the University of Chicago, she has partnered with communities across the country to improve school systems and life opportunities for youth. She has experience employing mixed methodology to help guide discussions and action in the arenas of teacher support and development, school leadership training, writing curricula for high-needs schools, and alternative high school pathways.


Frances earned her MA in Educational Policy at the University of California at Berkeley where she completed her graduate thesis on the process of improving opportunities for students through redefining the cultural context of school expectations, social structures, and parental roles. She has a BA in sociology with honors from the University of Chicago.
