Jeremy Nguyen

Project Facilitator

Jeremy Nguyen joined the Gardner Center as a Project Facilitator in June 2014. His role includes coordinating logistics and communications with the John W. Gardner Center's collaborative networks. For the last seven years, Jeremy has worked with a variety of nonprofit organizations in the Bay Area to improve the lives of youth in adverse economic environments. He has previously worked with AmeriCorps' Reading Partners to boost literacy rates for students in low-income households. He also brings experience in providing tutoring and curriculum development with the YMCA of Silicon Valley afterschool program and Embrace Kids Foundation. In his spare time, Jeremy provides counseling support for Vietnamese youth in college readiness and career advancement. Jeremy holds a BA in Child and Adolescent Development from San Jose State University and hopes to get his Masters in Higher Education.
