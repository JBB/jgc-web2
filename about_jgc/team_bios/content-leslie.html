Leslie Patron

Project Coordinator

Leslie Patron has been a Project Coordinator at the John W. Gardner Center since July of 2012. Her role is to coordinate the work of the College Readiness Indicator Systems Project, as well as the Youth Data Archive. She also contributes her expertise in communications and outreach to the Center’s communications team as Web and publications editor, and manager of the Gardner Center’s social media presence.


Leslie’s previous experience includes serving as the Project Manager for Communications and Outreach at the Rhode Island Afterschool Plus Alliance (RIASPA), where she worked to increase RIASPA’s membership and social media presence; wrote, formatted, and distributed a series of quarterly policy briefs; and oversaw a statewide initiative that allowed high-school youth to receive graduation credit for innovative collaborations with classroom teachers and community partners outside of the classroom. Prior to this, she served as a Research Project Assistant and Publications Associate at BTW informing change in Berkeley, CA where she managed the production and editing process for all of the company’s deliverables and worked to support research projects across a variety of content areas. Leslie holds an MFA in Literary Arts from Brown University and a BA in Creative Writing from San Francisco State University.
