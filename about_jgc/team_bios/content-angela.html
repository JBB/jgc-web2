Angela Ongoco

Project Coordinator

Angela Ongoco joined the John Gardner Center as a Project Coordinator in August 2010. She previously worked at the Elmezzi Foundation in New York City where she led a community-driven research and organizing initiative. Prior to that, she performed research on various global development topics at the household- and community-levels. She has lived and worked in Kenya for the Millennium Villages Project, China for the United Nations Development Fund for Women, and the Philippines for Innovations for Poverty Action. She has also worked in the private sector as a Research Associate for NERA Economic Consulting. In 2006, Angela graduated from Barnard College with a Bachelors Degree in Economics and Human Rights.
