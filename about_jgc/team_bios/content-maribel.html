Maribel Santiago

Graduate Research Assistant

Maribel Santiago is a part of the JGC team focused on the College Readiness Indicator Systems project. Prior to becoming a doctoral student at Stanford University's School of Education, Maribel was a lead teacher and program coordinator for the UCLA Institute for Democracy, Education, and Access's Council of Youth Research where she taught high school students graduate-level research techniques and worked with them in conducting education research. She also taught high school social studies for several years in Watts, where she created community-based lesson plans that encouraged students to learn about and work within their neighborhoods. She received her B.A.s in History and Chicana/o Studies and M.Ed from UCLA.
