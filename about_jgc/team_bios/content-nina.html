Nina Duong

Project Coordinator

Nina Duong is a Project Coordinator at the John W. Gardner Center. Her primary projects include the Community Schools Resource Initiative (CSRI) and the East Palo Alto Arts, Music, and Cultural Center.


Prior to joining the Gardner Center, Nina served as a research fellow for the Stanford Prevention Research Center, working on the evaluation of an occupational health program. She has also performed a community health assessment of school gardens in the Redwood City School District. For the past four years, Nina has been a member of several Stanford-affiliated education and health-promotion programs. Her interests include early childhood development, particularly the relationship between children's health status and academic outcomes.


Nina is a recent graduate from Stanford University with a Bachelors degree in Human Biology, concentrating in Child Development and Community Health.
