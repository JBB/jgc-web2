Driving to the Stanford campus


The closest visitor parking is in the Tresidder Union lot, #L-39.

Google Directions



Metered and "Pay and Display" parking are available at the Tresidder Union parking lot for a cost of $1.50 per hour. Parking meters require quarters and have a two-hour limit. "Pay and Display" machines accept Visa or MasterCard, cash, or coins, but do not issue change. Please be sure to place the "Pay and Display" receipt on your dashboard. Parking is FREE after 4:00 pm and during weekends.


Walking to Barnum Center from the Tresidder Union parking lot
  • Head north toward the center of campus with Tresidder Union on your left and Dinkelspiel Auditorium on your right.
  • Continue north on Lasuen Mall with the Old Union on your left and the Bookstore on your right.
  • Barnum Center is on your right before reaching the Clock Tower at 505 Lasuen Mall.

Alternate Parking in Parking Structure 6
  • If the Tresidder Union parking lot is full, return to Mayfield Drive and backtrack to Campus Drive.
  • Turn left on Campus Drive.
  • Turn left on Bowdoin Lane.
  • Look for the Parking Structure 6 sign.
  • Turn left on Wilbur Way.
  • Immediately turn right into the underground parking garage.
  • Visitor parking is on the first floor, look for the visitor parking ticket machines.

Walking to Barnum Center from Parking Structure 6
  • Exit the parking structure and turn left on Bowdoin Lane.
  • Cross Galvez Street and continue between the Law School (on your left) and CERAS (on your right).
  • Head diagonally across Canfield Court toward the Clock Tower, keeping the Bookstore to your left.
  • Enter through the back entrance of Barnum Center and walk through the building to JGC.

From the Palo Alto Caltrain stop, free Marguerite shuttles will take you to campus. Take the Palm Drive shuttle (Line P) and get off at the Oval.

Walking to Barnum Center from the Oval
  • Face the Main Quad.
  • Turn left and walk down Serra Mall.
  • Turn right on Lasuen Mall and walk the length of the Main Quad.
  • Pass the School of Education (Cubberley) and the Clock Tower on your left.
  • Barnum Center is on your left at 505 Lasuen Mall.
