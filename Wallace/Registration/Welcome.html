6 - 7 OCTOBER
From Data to Action

The Wallace Foundation is pleased to invite you to a two-day convening of grantees
and guests on October 6 and 7, 2015, at Stanford University. Hosted by the John W. Gardner Center
for Youth and Their Communities, the convening will focus on the strategic use of data to drive improvement and learning in
cross-sector community collaboratives. Key issues to be discussed include data use,
quality, governance, sustainability, and communications.

Participants will have an opportunity to apply creative problem solving to real scenarios their cities face.
