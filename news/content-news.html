  • Saturday, Oct 6, 2012 - 2:15pm - 3:15pm
    Standard of Excellence: How John W. Gardner Inspires a New Generation of Leadership

    Saturday, Oct 6, 2012 - 2:15pm - 3:15pm

    Standard of Excellence: How John W. Gardner Inspires a New Generation of Leadership

    Cemex Auditorium

    Stanford University


    Citizen, statesman and teacher, John W. Gardner (1912-2002) continues to inspire individuals and institutions seeking to advance the common good. Join us as a panel of national leaders reflect on the legacy of John W. Gardner at a forum marking the centennial of his birth. Robert L. Joss, Philip H. Knight Professor and Dean, Emeritus, Stanford Graduate School of Business will moderate the panel that includes:


    • Diana Aviv, President and CEO, Independent Sector

    • Robert Edgar, President and CEO, Common Cause

    • Marc Freedman, Founder and CEO, Civic Ventures

    • Milbrey McLaughlin, Founding Director, John W. Gardner Center

    • Timothy Wirth, President, United Nations Foundation and Better World Fund


    Hosted by the John W. Gardner Center for Youth and Their Communities, the Stanford School of Education, the Stanford Graduate School of Business, and the Haas Center for Public Service.
